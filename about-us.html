About Us

Debra Hall, Founder & CEO

Debra Hall is founder and CEO of RDM Business Solutions.  She is an accomplished business and accounting executive with over 20 years  experience developing financial control systems and accounting  processes for small to mid-sized companies. Holding positions from CFO  to Controller to Internal Operations Manager has helped to develop a  particular interest in improving financial accuracy and creating foundational systems to help plan for and accommodate business growth.


Debra's  education experience includes a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Accounting  from St. John’s University and A Professional Certificate in Finance  Management from Cornell University.


After  leaving the 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. to work as an independent consultant for  several years, RDM Business Solutions was formed. Referrals from repeat  and long-term clients created a need to expand and develop a team of  professional accountants and bookkeepers to help support business  leaders with their back-office functions, allowing them to focus on  their business. 


With  over 20 years of Accounting, Finance, and Human Resources she assists  her clients in all business and life aspects. Providing Daily Financial  Management and Organization Assistance for busy professionals, small  businesses and elderly, Budgeting and Spend Analysis, Expense Tracking,  and Tax Compliance
